Few and little with and without a
With **a** the meaning is positive. Without **a** the meaning is negative.

_A few customers_ = some customers, a small number of customers  
_Few customers_ = not many customers  
_A little progress_ = some progress, a small amount of progress  
_Little progress_ = not much progress
