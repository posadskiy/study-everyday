|                        | My                      | Mine            |
|------------------------|-------------------------|-----------------|
| First person singular  | It's my car.            | It's mine.      |
| Second person singular | Here's your coat.       | Here's yours.   |
| Third person singular  | That's his room.        | That's his.     |
|                        | The dog's got its food. | It's hers.      |
| First person plural    | That's our table.       | That's ours.    |
| Second person plural   | Are these your tickets? | Are these yours |

_Tom is a friend of mine._ (= one of my friends) not ~~a friend of me~~  
_Rachel came to the party with a cousin of hers._ (= one of her cousins)  
_I borrowed some magazines of yours._ (= some of your magazines)  

Note also 's in this example: _Rita is a friend of Melanie’s._
